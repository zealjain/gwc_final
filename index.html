What Fruitful Is

Have you ever been walking on a street and noticed a fruit tree in someone's backyard that is never picked, wishing you could pick from it? This is the exact problem Fruitful aims to fix by allowing neighbors to post fruit trees they own and allow fellow neighbors to pick the fruit. Our mission is to increase community presence in cities by creating a network of fruit trees while also reducing food waste.

How to Find Fruit Near You

1

Click on the "Pick" section at the top of the page. Type in any location in the search bar located at the top of the map or use the zoom function to navigate to your location.

2

Click on any of the nearby markers to get specific information such as fruit type, contact information, and notes. The location of the tree is indicated by the location of the red marker on the map. If you do not see any red location markers, there are no postings near you at this time.

3

Please respect any special instructions given by tree ownwer when visitng the location. Happy picking!

How to Post

1

Click on the "Pick" section at the top of the page.

2

Complete the information requested in the form. All fields are required. If you do not wish to disclose your exact location you may enter your zipcode.

3

Click the post button to post.
