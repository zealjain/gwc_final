What Fruitful Is

Have you ever been walking on a street and noticed a fruit tree in someone's backyard that is never picked, wishing you could pick from it? This is the exact problem Fruitful aims to fix by allowing neighbors to post fruit trees they own and allow fellow neighbors to pick the fruit. Our mission is to increase community presence in cities by creating a network of fruit trees while also reducing food waste.

How to Pick

1

Click on the "Pick" section at the top of the page to find fruit near you.

2

Find your area on the map and click on any of the nearby markers to get specific information. If you do not see any red location markers, there are no postings near you at this time.

3

Please respect any special instructions given by tree ownwer when visitng the location. By using our website you are agreeing to our terms and consitions. Happy picking!

How to Post

1

Click on the "Post" section at the top of the page.

2

Complete the information requested in the form. If you do not wish to disclose your exact location you may enter your zipcode. Remember that all information you enter will be publically available.

3

Click the post button to post. By posting you are agreeing to our terms and conditions.
