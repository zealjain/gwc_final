Who We Are

Fruitful is an organization where you can share fruit with people in your local community. We value community and sharing, and believe that with this organization people can become better aqquainted with their neighbors promoting a greater sense of community all over the world. This program was started by a group of high school students commmitted to making a change in their community. Now, we hope to promote more people to garden and get the nutrition of home grown foods rather than ones that you find in the stores.

How to Pick

1

Make sure you are signed into your account or create an account. Remember that you have to be signed into your account in order to pick fruit. Navigate to the top of the page and click on the "Pick" section, to find fruit near you!

2

Once you reach the pick page you will see a map with premarked locations. You can click the markers for more information on that pick location like the fruit type and special instructions, however you can also navigate to the serach bar and type a location and find the fruit nearest to you.

3

Once you type a location in the search bar, the page will inform you where the closest tree or source of fruit is, according to the location that you entered.

How to Post

1

Make sure you are signed into your account, or create an account. You cannot post without having an account. Navigate to the top of the page and click on the "Post" section, you can use this to post about any fruit you have or public fruit trees you know of!

2

Fill out information in the form. Some pieces of information are required but others are optional. Remember that you don't have to disclose your location you can always enter a park name or organize a fruit drop off at a local park. You also have the option of providing you zipcode and having people contact you privately. Please keep in mind that all information you enter will be publically avalible.

3

Click the post button to post. By posting you are agreeing to our terms and conditions. You can always click the back button to return this page.
